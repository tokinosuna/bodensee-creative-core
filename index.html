Talent is Born Here.

Careers Should Be Too.

The Bodensee region is a crucible of talent. But where do our creators go? They leave. We're here to change that narrative.

We are losing our most valuable resource: Our People.

The Brain Drain

Our schools train world-class digital artists, developers, and designers. Yet, many seek opportunities in major cities, believing a creative career isn't viable here.

The Community Gap

Existing organizations are valuable, but often don't speak the language of the new generation of digital-native, often introverted, creators.

The Hidden Barriers

For female and gender-diverse creators, the hurdles are even higher. They face a lack of role models, networking opportunities, and a safe space to navigate challenges.

Our mission is to build a self-sustaining creative ecosystem.

We do this through three core pillars:

Inspiration & Visibility

Featuring local role models through our **Creator Spotlights** and building a community from scratch with our TikTok project, Cloubitt's Corner.

Skills & Resources

Providing curated **Learning Roadmaps** and an **Open Toolbox** of free software to lower the barrier of entry for every aspiring creator.

Connection & Opportunity

Building a public **Creator Directory**, fostering our **Ctrl+S Sisterhood** initiative, and sharing local jobs and projects.
